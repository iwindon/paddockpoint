Community Pool Information

Location: 123 Paddock Point Drive

Season: May 15 – September 15

Hours: 8 AM – 9 PM daily

Pool Rules

  • No lifeguard on duty — swim at your own risk.
  • Children under 14 must be accompanied by an adult.
  • No glass containers.
  • Proper swimwear required.
  • Key fob required for entry.
