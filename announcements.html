How to Report a Non-Emergency Safety Concern

If you observe concerning activity in the neighborhood, please use the following steps to make a clear report.

  1. For immediate danger or urgent situations, call 911.
  2. For non-emergency concerns, call the Spartanburg County non-emergency line at 864-596-2222.
  3. Provide the location and a brief factual description of what you observed.
  4. The non-emergency number connects you to an operator, who will route the call and dispatch the appropriate response.
  5. Depending on the situation, follow-up may be by phone, or an officer/deputy may be sent to your home if needed.
  6. If you have additional neighborhood concerns, you may also notify the HOA Board for documentation.
