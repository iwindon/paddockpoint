July 11, 2026

Procedure for notifying Police on non-emergency issues

I reached out to the Spartanburg Sherrifs office to ask about what needs to be done about reports of drug activity in the neighborhood. They said to one report the issue to your HOA for documentation purposes. You also then need to contact the non-emergency line at 864-596-2222 to report the issue. They will then turn it over to their narcotics department for further investigation. If you need someone to come out right away let them know and a deputy will be dispatched. If it's an emergency, call 911. If you have any questions, please reach out to the HOA board.
